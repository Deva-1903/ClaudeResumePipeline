# Summer 2026 Internship at Wintermute Ventures

**Company:** Wintermute Ventures (investment arm of Wintermute)
**Location:** New York / Singapore / Remote
**Team:** Ventures
**Duration:** 8-12 weeks, full-time, Summer 2026

## About Wintermute

At Wintermute our mission is to enable, empower and advance the truly decentralized world for more transparent and efficient markets and products. We provide liquidity algorithmically across most trading venues in crypto, supporting all major centralized and decentralized trading venues, AMMs, RFQs, aggregators and chains. Wintermute is also offering best-in-class OTC products to crypto native as well as traditional financial institutions.

Wintermute is actively participating in the building and development of the blockchain ecosystem through investments from Wintermute Ventures, partnerships and co-development with upcoming protocols and incubation of own projects. We act as official liquidity providers for a large number of the world's highest profile blockchain projects.

## About Wintermute Ventures

Wintermute Ventures is the investment arm of Wintermute. We back early stage companies and protocols across DeFi, infrastructure, applications, market structure and emerging ecosystems.

We invest with the benefit of Wintermute's deep market access, trading expertise, and connectivity across the crypto ecosystem. Since 2020, Wintermute Ventures has invested in more than 120 projects. Alongside our venture portfolio, we also manage a substantial liquid token portfolio across L1s, L2s, DeFi, infrastructure, and new ecosystems.

This role is ideal for someone who is genuinely interested in crypto markets and enjoys working at the intersection of fundamental research, market structure, token analysis, and investment judgment.

## About the role

University students across Bachelors, Masters, MBA, or other postgraduate programs in Tech, Business, Sciences, Finance, Economics or a related field. Meaningful and current personal experience in crypto required; graduating this year or 2027 at the latest.

Remote internship; resulting full-time offer based in New York or Singapore (valid work permit required).
