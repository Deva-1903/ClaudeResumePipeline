## Your Impact Starts Here

We're looking for an AI Engineering Intern who loves mapping real workflows, breaking down ambiguous problems, and shipping AI automations that directly improve how our internal teams operate. In this role, you'll embed with partner teams, learn how their work gets done, and rapidly experiment with LLM-powered solutions that drive measurable impact.

This internship is a fit if you're excited about AI-first engineering, fast iteration, and operating at the front lines of the business. You'll help build the playbooks and tools that shape how Homebase adopts AI across the company.

### Key ways you'll contribute and create impact

- Embed with internal teams (e.g., Sales, Marketing, Support) to map workflows, surface pain points, and identify high-leverage automation opportunities.
- Design, prototype, and ship AI-powered automations end-to-end, applying LLMs creatively to real business workflows in ambiguous, fast-changing environments.
- Build full-stack solutions — from Python scripts and backend services to intuitive React interfaces — for internal teams to use in production.
- Operate like a product-minded engineer: run interviews, co-design solutions, and iterate based on real-world usage and feedback.
- Measure impact rigorously — ship changes that move metrics like rep talk time, ticket deflection, adoption, and operational efficiency.
- Codify what works by creating reusable templates, playbooks, and documentation for future engineers and partner teams.
- Experiment with AI tools to accelerate your own workflows and demonstrate AI fluency in your daily work.

- Passion for helping small businesses and building tools that make teams more effective.
